Form Endpoints for Static Sites

Netlify

Form handling and so much more. CDN, Continuous deployment, 1 click HTTPS. From instant prototypes to huge global enterprises.

Categories: static sites, form endpoints. Pricing: free and paid ($9/month +)

Formspree

Functional HTML forms. Just send your form to Formspree's URL and it will forward to your email. No PHP, Javascript or sign up required — perfect for static sites!

Categories: static sites, form endpoints. Pricing: free and paid ($9.99/month)

Formkeep

From the creators of Bourbon. Form endpoints for designers and developers. No iframes, JavaScript embeds, or CSS overrides.

Categories: static sites, form endpoints. Pricing: Free and $39/month

Formbucket

Endpoints are just the beginning. Form handling and automation for web designers and developers.

Categories: static sites, form endpoints. Pricing: Free

Formsubmit

Simple backend for your forms in static sites. Formsubmit sends all your form submissions to your email. Coding ability optional.

Categories: static sites, form endpoints. Pricing: Free

GetForm

Simple effortless form processing. Form endpoints to manage the backend of your online webforms.

Categories: static sites, form endpoints. Pricing: Free
